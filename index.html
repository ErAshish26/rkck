Welcome to

Rajasthan Government Health Scheme

Family is a beneficial health insurance plan for beneficiary category including Ministers, MLAs, ex-MLAs, All India Services, Serving and Retired employees of State Government and of State Autonomous Bodies. All Family beneficiary will have access to the cashless medical facility based on the Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS) package rates as per the applicable medical rules for the respective Family category . Some of the features and benefits of the scheme are outlined as given below:

Self Managed Family e-card system for beneficiary
Provision of Electronic Medical Record(EMR)
Cashless Hospitalization & OPD Medicines
Wide Coverage of Medical Facility
Automated IT enabled Process
Services outside Rajasthan
Enhanced Medical Facility
Exclusion features

Cashless Hospitalization & OPD Medicines

Cashless Hospitalization: The beneficiaries of Family can avail cashless hospitalization in all Government and duly empanelled private hospitals of the State. The complete list is available on Family web-portal: www.Family.rajasthan.gov inand as the name suggests, a cashless treatment means that the beneficiary does not have to bear the cost of a medical treatment. The costs will be borne by the government. Cashless facility shall be offered only at empanelled Health care Network providers (HCNP) including all Government and Private hospitals, Diagnostic / Imaging centers under Family at the prescribed CGHS rates.

Cashless OPD Medicines: The beneficiaries of Family can avail OPD cashless medicines from Government owned CONFED stores as well as from Private empanelled Pharma Stores. The complete list is available on Family web-portal:www.Family.rajasthan.gov.in. Family beneficiary will also be able to upload the OPD prescription with the given facility on their registered SSO login ID along with a “VIEW PHARMACY” option to see the list of Pharma stores as per the District and City location.

Enhanced Medical Facility

A large number of Empanelled Hospitals and Pharma stores: To provide maximum benefits to all the beneficiaries a large number of private hospitals and Pharma stores are empanelled under the scheme across the State. This will help to cater the patients at the time of medical emergency.

Empanelment of Diagnostic Centre/ Imaging centre: All Family Beneficiaries can also avail the services of especially diagnostic centres/ Imaging centres for investigations and tests for easy and hassle Family diagnosis of the disease as an added feature in medical facilities along with novel e – pharma facility. This will bring all the health coverage facilities under one umbrella for imparting holistic treatment facilties. City location.

Wide Coverage of Medical Facility

Wide Coverage of Medical facilities: Family offers a comprehensive package of treatment offering coverage that pays for the entire cycle of medical emergency as well as for OPD/IPD/Day Care, maternity and Child care facility. All the packages and rates will be as per the CGHS guidelines and therefore Beneficiary does not have to pay the hospital private designed rates.

Family Card Tracker System: All Family beneficiary have an accessibility of Family card Tracker facility on their registered SSO login ID which will have a feature of OPD and IPD card limit display as per their applicable category.

facility for Pensioner’s: Family beneficiary falling under the Pensioner category (under RCS (Pension) Rules 1996) can also avail the facility of online OPD enhancement, an easy and hassle Family system developed to provide maximum benefit so that they can have a real time provision of the availability of the funds for the required medicines.

Facility For SAB (serving and Retired employees): For Family beneficiaries, especially SAB serving and Retired employees a sum insurance of Rs 5 lakhs (Rs. 20,000 OPD inclusive) will be admissible per family per year with an extra sum of Rs 5 lakhs coverage per family per year for any catastrophic illness as specified within the scheme.

Automated IT enabled Process

Automated IT enabled facility: Enhancing e-governance in the State, Family includes a completely automated IT enabling facility for Seamless Registration, Healthcare Network Provider (HCNP) empanelment, Beneficiary Identification Selection (BIS), Family Package selection for treatment, Transaction Management and Claim settlement and Grievance redressal mechanism making it a complete paperless system. This will have minimum documentation at each level and will provide a hassle Family admission, treatment and discharge to the Family beneficiary for any medical treatment at a hospital.

Online processing of HCNP Empanelment: The entire Private Health care network Providers (HCNP) can use the online empanelment module for applying for empanelment under Family through the Family web portal. The selection process for approval/rejection of the HCNP application by the Health benefit Empowered Committee(HBEC) will also work on online and automated module in a time bound manner. The Empanelment of private Pharma Store is also an online empanelment module.

Services outside Rajasthan

Besides private hospital empanelment in the State of Rajasthan, few selected multi-specialty / Specialty private hospitals are also registered under the scheme outside the State enabling the beneficiaries to opt for the medical facilities in case of dire need whenever required outside Rajasthan.

Provision of Electronic Medical Record(EMR)

Family beneficiary will have its own electronic medical record for easy access to the medical history and to avail the best medical facilities in selected State Government and empanelled private Hospitals.

Self Managed Family e-card system for beneficiary

Self Printing of Family e-card: Since Family is an automated IT enabled module, beneficiaries can print their own Family e-card on Family web-portal: www.Family.rajasthan.gov.in to avail the medical facility.

Add-on/Deletion of dependent family members as beneficiary:All Family beneficiaries will have an access to any change in add-on/ deletion feature of dependent Family members using SSO login Id and Edit module on Family web-portal.

Single Family Card Number All the Eligible dependent Family members of Family card holder will have a single unique ID number printed on the Family card.

Exclusion features

There are certain hospital / medical treatments and non payable items which are not included within the scheme and are not covered in the claims. These conditions and features are as per enlisted in CGHS guidelines.
